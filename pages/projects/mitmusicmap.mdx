[MIT Music Map](https://trudypainter.github.io/mit-music-map/) was my final group project for [CMS.405 (Visual Design)](https://cms405.github.io/).
I worked with Hanu Park and Rachel Chae.

My group designed and implemented an interactive data visualization of where MIT students listen to songs on campus

![](/mitmusicmap/map.gif)

My primary role in the group was as a software developer. I adapted code from Dan Shiffman's
[Autonomous Agents Chapter](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLRqwX-V7Uu6YHt0dtyf4uiw8tKOxQLvlW)
from [_The Nature of Code_](https://natureofcode.com/book/chapter-6-autonomous-agents/).

Below is a diagram of the logic behind the autonmous vehicles in the MIT Music Map.

![](/mitmusicmap/steeringprog.png)

I wrote the code for this website using p5.js. You can find it in [this repository](https://github.com/trudypainter/mit-music-map).

Access [MIT Music Map](https://trudypainter.github.io/mit-music-map/).
